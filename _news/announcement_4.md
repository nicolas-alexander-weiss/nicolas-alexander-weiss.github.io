In an interdisciplinary collobaration between the fields of non-linear algebra and cosmology with <a href="https://sites.google.com/de-korte.tel/claire-de-korte/home">Claire de Korte</a>, <a href="https://inspirehep.net/authors/1898657">Harry Goodhew </a>, and <a href="https://inspirehep.net/authors/1879379">Kamran Salehi Vaziri</a>, we have been exploring how Kronecker products can be used to obtain graph-local weight-shifting matrices for Feynman diagrams in de Sitter spacetime.
